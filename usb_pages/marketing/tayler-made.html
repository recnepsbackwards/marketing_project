Let your brand be the first thing they see when they open the mailbox

Ditch the normal newsletter and step up to a high quality printed Wealth Magazine.

Wealth Managers and financial advisors share one thing in common: They need low-cost, big-image marketing tools to keep their brand in front of clients year-round.

If you’ve explored marketing ideas, you already know that this is no easy task. To send a newsletter, you usually need a designer, a writer and big budget – plus a lot of extra time to manage the process.

Fortunately, there’s a better way with the Tailor-Made™ Magazine custom marketing tool! Now, you can send your clients a professional, completely branded magazine – with your logo on the cover and your letter on the first page. It’s more professional than having your own custom newsletter, plus a whole lot easier and more affordable!

Here are five great features you’ll love:

Positive, uplifting articles that appeal to all types of clients. Articles are professionally written and edited so you never have to worry about sourcing content. Each edition includes articles about travel, golf, philanthropic endeavors, books, technology tips, a delicious recipe and more. Because the articles are not time-sensitive, your magazine will enjoy a long shelf life.

Completely customizable magazine title, cover, back cover (hyperlink to our print ad section) and first page letter. We offer varying degrees of customization to fit every company’s unique needs. See pricing below:



No ads. Unlike similar products, our custom marketing magazines contain NO advertising. This means you never have to worry about clients receiving an unintended sales pitch.

No financial content. Although many professionals use this custom marketing tool, it does not contain any financial or ghost written articles. This helps minimize compliance and legal reviews.

Seamless service – requires only 30 minutes of your time each quarter. Once your magazine template is established, you can put your marketing program on auto-pilot. Simply place your order, upload your custom letter to your account and upload your current mailing list.
