Brand Identity

Your brand identity is how you envision your clients or customers to perceive your business. Each element of your brand plays a different role, that together, make up an identity unlike any other. The typical components of a brand identity are:

  • Company name
  • Unique logo
  • Tagline
  • Typeface

These are the typical components of your identity, but a strong brand can be determined by many other factors including tone, behavior, business focus, etc. A well-built identity with strategy and forethought will communicate your company’s personality, products, and value, helping you build long-lasting recognition amongst your clients and peers, as well as creating a loyal following and giving consumers a reason to buy products from you.

If you want consumers to care about your brand, it needs an identity.
