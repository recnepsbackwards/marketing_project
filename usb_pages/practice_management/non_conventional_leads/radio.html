Radio Appearances

They tune in to hear what the host and guests are talking about daily. The voices heard by the listener are often regarded as coming from old friends or even a trusted advisor. That's why the established relationship that hosts have with their listeners can constitute a strong third-party endorsement for you and is also why it can be an excellent promotional strategy to provide broad and immediate national exposure for you or your book.

Here are some facts about our radio campaign and the guidelines we use:

  • All interviews are done over the phone, in the comfort of the spokesperson's home or office.
  • Another guideline we use is scheduling interviews on stations boasting a minimum of 5,000 watts of power.
  • The spokesperson will have an opportunity to direct listeners to your web site and/or stores where your book can be purchased.
  • Due to our close relationships with hosts and producers, we know the shows that are a good match for your topic.

Want to get your message out on Radio? Radio ads can be great way to reach your target audience. Radio spots can be announcer read only or can be produced with music, talent etc. Lengths of 15 seconds, 30 seconds or 60 seconds. Spots can be produced as part of a series or as stand alones.
