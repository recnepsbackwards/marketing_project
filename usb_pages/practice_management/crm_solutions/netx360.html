NetX360

NetX360® is Pershing’s digitally enabled professional platform for broker-dealers, wealth managers and advisors. It is designed to help you work more efficiently, onboard and collaborate with clients more effectively, and stay connected on the go. It helps you easily manage accounts, deliver first-rate service and develop new revenue opportunities. Our flexible technology gives you easy access to a wealth of market-leading solutions from Pershing and over 250 third-party providers for CRM, financial planning, research and much more.

My Dashboard is NetX360's highly customizable home page. You can tailor the platform's content and navigation to the way you conduct your business. Build and rename dashboard pages and tabs; drag and drop content tiles to create relevant displays of information; and modify navigation and content hierarchy.

From My Dashboard, you can also access a host of tools to make navigation easy, including:

  • Global Search speeds your ability to find information.
  • Split Screen, making it easier to view categories such as balances, trades, and performance and the detail behind them.
  • Quick views of summary or detailed account information.
  • Access to information available through subscription or integration with other systems, including CRM, Market News and more.

Fully integrated into NetX360, our Client Onboarding solution makes it easy to complete every step needed to onboard a new client—from populating data to generating forms to obtaining signatures and funding accounts. Our seamless solution can help you:

  • Simplify your client onboarding process with one click.
  • Reduce Not in Good Order (NIGO) rates.
  • Obtain instant access.
  • Gather verifiable, secure e-signatures.
  • Fund accounts faster and achieve higher funding rates.
