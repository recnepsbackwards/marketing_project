DST Vision

Compiling information manually from a number of different sources is time-consuming, error prone, and inefficient. Our advisor portal is an automated, single source of customer account information from mutual funds, annuities, and alternative investments that simplifies the aggregation process and drives accuracy and cost-savings. Advisors and broker-dealers benefit from a more complete view into their book of business and their clients’ portfolios through real-time, premium, dealer-branded, consolidated reports that help meet regulatory requirements and promote better service.

Account Management. Simplified. Financial intermediaries no longer have to contact management companies separately. Vision delivers two highly sought-after capabilities for increasing client management efficiency: name-based navigation across an advisor’s book-of-business and presentation of account information through systematic groupings of clients and households. Protected by seven levels of security access, these innovations, among others, are continually enhanced to simplify account management and serve the evolving needs of financial intermediaries.

Key Features:

  • Book-of-business dashboard activity aggregated across their participating product companies.
  • Real-time access to account details housed in DST’s recordkeeping platform.
  • Transaction activity monitoring and alerts.
  • Systematic grouping of accounts by client and household.
  • Client-ready reports, ranging from lists to detailed reports of holdings.
  • Automatic grouping of accounts by retirement, non-retirement, education, health care, etc.
  • Comprehensive account details: registration, cost basis, tax summary information, and more.
