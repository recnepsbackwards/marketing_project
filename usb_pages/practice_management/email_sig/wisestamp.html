Inside View

WiseStamp was founded in 2010 with one clear goal: helping customers succeed in business. WiseStamp equips users with simple, yet resourceful tools and services to instantly help them smartly and professionally market themselves and grow their business online.

The company currently has over 700,000 users and tens of thousands of paying customers. Users send a combined 150 million emails a month using the platforms dynamic signature product.

We've created a vast variety of templates, apps and designs to fit any type of business. WiseIntro was introduced to bring WiseStamp's vision of providing micro-businesses with tools that were previously too expensive or inaccessible to fruition.
