Women's Path

In the last few decades, the number of women in the U.S. labor force has increased considerably. Currently, 60% of women are employed outside of the home, up from 37% in 1960 (Population Reference Bureau, 2001). As a result of this development, retirement income planning is more relevant to women than ever before.

Women’s Path to Retirement Income is a new system Asset designed to educate and empower women about financial issues they may face at some point in their lives. The system provides a strategy for targeting the vast, largely untapped women’s market. Addressing all single, married, divorced and widowed women ages 45-65, the scripting that accompanies this system gives your female audience real-life examples of financial issues they may face. You can then provide substantive information they can use to stay in control of their assets throughout their retirement years.

Following our step-by-step process will show you how to enter this underserviced market and create unique educational events and workshops for women. You’ll learn to make use of your relationships with your female clients and women in your community to request quality referrals. You’ll also gather leads by using an optimized mailing campaign that’s “women friendly,” incorporating restaurants and menus and topics that may appeal more to women.
