Roth IRA

Based on input from several of our top producers from around the country, Asset worked to develop a system that examines the key attributes of a Roth and provides important information for those considering a Roth conversion.

The “Roth Puzzle System” looks at Roth as a potential piece of the “Retirement Income Puzzle.” Since the pieces of the retirement puzzle are interconnected – taxes, Social Security, Health care, lifestyle expenses, and more – we wanted to provide our producers’ clients and prospects with information to determine if a Roth fits into their Retirement Puzzle.

The Roth System includes the following components:

  • FINRA-reviewed PowerPoint presentation and script
  • Client brochure
  • Mail piece

The Roth Puzzle System discusses:

  • Comparison of taxes paid (contributions and withdrawals) for a traditional IRA vs. Roth
  • Basic attributes (including penalties, RMDs, etc.) of a traditional IRA vs. Roth
  • Rules for contributing to a Roth and Roth conversions
  • Historical and Current tax rates
  • Various sources of retirement income and which are considered taxable income
  • Sources of income that can trigger taxation of Social Security and impact Medicare premiums
  • Key questions when considering a Roth conversion
