Social Security Puzzle

Do your Clients and Prospects know that delaying their Social Security to age 70 can mean a 76% higher benefit amount than if they started claiming at age 62? Are they aware that Social Security may be their most valuable retirement asset, potentially worth anywhere from $400,000 to over $1 million for a couple if they receive benefits for an average of 20 years?

The Social Security Puzzle System provides you with all the tools you need to help prospects and clients optimize their Social Security decisions. Addressing Social Security as an important piece of the retirement income puzzle establishes you as a trusted financial professional who can help prospects and clients with all aspects of income planning for retirement. Additional steps have been taken to have the direct mail pieces, a PowerPoint presentation and scripting reviewed by FINRA.
