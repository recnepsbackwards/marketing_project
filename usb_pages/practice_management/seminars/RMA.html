Risk Management Analysis

Think of the retirement income planning process as having two components. The first is the actual retirement income plan to provide predictable, reliable, sustainable, inflation-adjusted income in retirement.

The second part is managing potential risks to the retirement plan, such as a health care/long-term care issue or early death of a spouse.

The Risk Management Analysis System (or RMA) looks at the threats that could prevent the Client from enjoying a successful retirement; you can then identify possible solutions to address those risks.
